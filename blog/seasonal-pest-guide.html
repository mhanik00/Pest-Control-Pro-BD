Seasonal Pest Guide: What to Watch For

Different seasons bring different pest challenges. Understanding seasonal pest patterns helps you better protect your home throughout the year.

Spring (March - May)

  • Ants begin foraging
  • Termite swarmers emerge
  • Mosquitoes become active
  • Wasps start building nests

Summer (June - August)

  • Peak mosquito activity
  • Increased ant presence
  • Flies and cockroaches thrive
  • Spider populations grow

Monsoon (July - October)

  • Increased termite activity
  • Mosquito breeding intensifies
  • Rodents seek shelter indoors
  • Higher risk of bed bug infestations

Winter (December - February)

  • Rodents move indoors for warmth
  • Cockroaches seek shelter
  • Stored product pests in pantries
  • Some insects hibernate

Prevention Tips for All Seasons

  • Regular inspection of your property
  • Maintain proper sanitation
  • Seal entry points
  • Schedule preventive treatments
