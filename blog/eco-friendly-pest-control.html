Eco-Friendly Pest Control Methods

Environmentally conscious pest control is becoming increasingly important. Learn about effective methods that protect both your home and the environment.

Natural Pest Control Methods

1. Biological Control

Using natural predators and parasites to control pest populations without harmful chemicals.

2. Plant-Based Solutions

  • Neem oil for various insects
  • Citrus oils for ants and roaches
  • Eucalyptus oil for flies and mosquitoes
  • Peppermint oil for rodents

Physical Control Methods

  • Sticky traps for crawling insects
  • UV light traps for flying insects
  • Mechanical barriers and screens
  • Diatomaceous earth for various pests

Cultural Control Practices

  • Proper sanitation and cleanliness
  • Moisture control
  • Proper food storage
  • Regular maintenance and inspection

Benefits of Eco-Friendly Pest Control

  • Safe for family and pets
  • No harmful chemical residues
  • Protects beneficial insects
  • Long-term sustainability
